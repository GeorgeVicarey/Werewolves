Original Game - Werewolves of Millers Hollow (The Pact)

What is Werewolves?

This is a social game involving cards that is very easy to learn and fun to play. Usually played outside BYOC, or in the social games area.

Who organizes it?

It’s normally myself, or Henners. Though there are a fair few people who play, and will organise games.

A simple explanation

All players sit in a circle and each player is given a single card at random by the moderator. They will look at this card, remember it and then place it face down in front of them.

The card you have will determine your team. Your team will be either werewolves or villagers. It is the villagers job to find and kill all the werewolves, and it is the werewolves job to eat all the villagers. Either way you win or lose as a team, the tricky part is you don’t know who is on your team and who is not.

The game progresses with phases, with each phase lasting a few minutes. The phases are called day and night.

Night Phase

During the night phase, all players will remain silent and close their eyes. The werewolves will be asked by the moderator to “wake up” (i.e Open their eyes in silence) and then point to the person they wish to eat. Their vote must be unanimous. They then close their eyes again as if nothing happened. The game opens with a night phase. If you open your eyes without being explicitly asked by the moderator, you will be mod-killed and removed from the game.

Day Phase

During the day phase, all players will discuss who they think are werewolves and vote on a single person to be lynched. This is done by the village making a number of nominations. It’s normally 3, but when fewer people are left, it is often dropped to 2. Once all nominations have been made, each nominee will be permitted to defend themself. A majority vote is then taken by the Moderator.

That is generally how the game progresses, each player upon being lynched or eaten will reveal their card to the group (in silence) and then will no longer participate in the game. The game ends when all werewolves or villagers are dead.

The Fun Part

There are a few more roles than just simple villager, or werewolf.
These roles are selected, balanced, and distributed by the moderator before starting the game.

Role List

  • Villager
    This is a simple villager. You are awake during the day, and you sleep through the night.
  • Werewolf
    The enemy of the village. You want to eat every villager, and win the game.

Pro-Village cards

  • Hunter
    The hunter is armed with a single shot. When the hunter dies, they immediately shoot someone by pointing with them. You may not discuss who to shoot.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Very common
  • Seer
    The seer wakes up in the night and is asked to choose a person to see the card of. The moderator then reveals their card.
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Very common
  • Elder
    The elder has seen a werewolf before. They have two lives against wolf kills.
    If the village lynches the elder during the day phase, all pro-village power roles listed here become inert and may no longer use their powers.
    Wake up in the night: Maybe, mod discretion.
    Common
  • Defender
    The defender wakes up in the night, and selects one person to protect from the werewolves. You may not choose the same person twice in a row, and you may choose to defend yourself.
    Wake up in the night: Yes.
    Very common
  • Witch
    The witch has two potions. A save potion, and a kill potion. They may only use each once per game.
    The person targetted by the werewolves is revealed, and the witch is asked if they would like to save them. They either nod their head, or shake their head to signal. They may only save someone once per game.
    The witch is then asked if they would like to kill anyone. If so, they silently point. If not, they shake their head. They may only kill someone once per game.
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Very common
  • Cupid
    Cupid awakes only once, during the very first night phase.
    Cupid selects two players to fall in love. The two players are tapped on the head, and are asked to wake up. They then reveal their cards to each other.
    If one of the lovers dies, the other lover also dies.
    If the lovers are of the same team (Village-Village or Werewolf-Werewolf), then no additional team is created.
    If the lovers are a combination of Villager / Werewolf, they immediately form a third, additional team with the win condition of being the last two players left alive. This is very difficult to accomplish, but is incredibly fulfilling when it occurs.
    Wake up in the night: No, only the first night phase.
    Common
  • Thief (Mode 1)
    The thief is presented with a set of 3 cards, from which they may choose one.
    That becomes their new role, and replaces their thief card in front of them.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare
  • Thief (Mode 2)
    The thief wakes up every other night, and is permitted to swap their card with another players.
    Each player must check their card in the day phase after every thief action.
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Rare
  • Scapegoat
    If the draw is ever tied, the scapegoat dies instead, regardless of if they are nominated or not.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare
  • Village Idiot
    If the village idiot is selected to be lynched, they do not die. Their card is revealed, and left face up. They may not vote in any lynch.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare
  • Fox
    The fox wakes up during the night, and selects a villager.
    The mod then signals if this villager, or either of their two alive neighbors is a wolf
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Rare
  • Bear Tamer
    If the bear tamer is in play, the moderator will make the noise of a bear during the start of the day phase if either alive neighbor of the bear tamer is a wolf.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare
  • Fearmonger/Beastmaster
    This role wakes up during the night, and selects a player. That player is nominated with one vote against them. They have priority in selecting the next nomination.
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Uncommon
  • Three Brothers
    There are three of these cards, and they all wake up together. They may silently communicate between each other.
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Uncommon
  • Twin Sisters
    Same as the Three Brothers, except there are two. (Unless Nanaki is playing, in which case there is only one. <3 u)
    Wake up in the night: Yes
    Uncommon
  • Devoted Servant
    The devoted servant may activate their ability when any person is lynched by the village.
    They must proclaim that they are the servant, reveal their card, and take the card of the person destined to be lynched, secretly look at it, and resume playing as that role.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare
  • Rusty Knight
    When the Rusty Knight is killed by Werewolves, the first werewolf to their left is killed the following night.
    Wake up in the night: No
    Rare

Pro-Werewolf cards

  • Big Bad wolf
    The Big, Bad wolf is on the side of the werewolves.
    They wakes up with the regular werewolves and selects a villager.
    They also wakes up on his own to eat an additional villager every night until there is a wolf death. This may or may not include the wolf hound, dependant on the mod.
    Rare
  • Wolf-Father
    The wolf father may convert non-wolf characters, and have them join the werewolf team.
    This is done by silently raising their hand after the mod asks for werewolves to sleep.
    When this is done, there is no confirmation. The mod silently touches the converted person on the head.
    The converted party joins the werewolf team retains their original powers and card.
    Uncommon
  • Wolf-Hound
    The wolf hound may choose, during the first night phase only, if they wish to be pro-village, or pro-wolf.
    They choose by waking up with the wolves, or not.
    Uncommon
  • Wild Child
    The wild child wakes up in the first night phase and selects a role model.
    When the role model is killed, the wild child joins the werewolves.
    Uncommon

Loaners

Each of these roles is their own team, with their own win condition

  • White Wolf
    Their goal is to be the last player left alive at the end of the game.
    The White Wolf wakes up on his own every other night and selects a werewolf to eat.
    Uncommon
  • Piper
    Their goal is to have everyone in the village mesmerised.
    The Piper wakes alone each night, and selects two people to mesmerise.
    Those two people are tapped on the head, and are told to wake up.
    Then, all other mesmerised people are awoken.
    Rare
  • Angel
    Their goal is to be lynched during the first day phase by the village.
    If they do, the game is over, angel victory.
    If they do not, they join the village team.
    Common
